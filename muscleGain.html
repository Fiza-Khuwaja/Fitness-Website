Before Starting . Always keep in mind these 3 things :-

BREATHING

Knowing how and when to breadth during a workout can make a huge difference in terms of your rep count and ability to lift heavier . "If you exhale all your air going down in a heavy squat you may not be able to come back up . But on the contrary if you exhale going up on your squat , you'er going to be able to deliver more power , which is going to increase your repetitions . And if you'er increasing repetitions ,you'll be able to build more strength and mucsle " . Ensure you are breadthing in through your nose filling up your diagphrams and breadthing out from mouth , emptying your diagphrams .

ANATOMY

Having a better unerstanding of your own body and how each muccle works is the key to maxamizing the effectiveness of your workout . "Without a proper understanding of your physiology and anatomy , you can be walking into a gym thinking that you'er going to get big arms just by doing dumbbell curls , not understanding that there's many other muscle groups that make up an arm is defenitely going to get you no where " . So having a good anatomy of your body is definitely help you progress faster

NUTRITION

Nutrition can be defined as food at work in the body. It can also be defined as the process by which the organism ingests, digests, absorbs, transports and utilizes nutrients and disposes of their end-products. Nutrition is an important aspect of each individual’s life. It is imperative to ensure that each one of us gets a balanced nutritional diet with all components that are needed in our stage of life.
